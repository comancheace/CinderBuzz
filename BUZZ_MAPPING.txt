Recommended Buzz MIDI CC mapping
================================

Use your Buzz MIDI mapping / MIDI tracker / Peer Control workflow to map these:

CC74 - Main filter cutoff
CC71 - Filter resonance
CC91 - Reverb send / reverb wet
CC93 - Delay send / delay wet
CC7  - Master FX amount / group volume
CC20 - Both hands up trigger. Use for build/drop macro if available.
CC21 - Clap trigger. Use for one-shot FX or sample trigger if available.

Musical advice
--------------
For a modern psytrance song, use the Kinect mostly for automation:
- Left hand up: open the bass/lead filter before a drop.
- Right hand up: add reverb during breakdowns.
- Move hands apart: increase riser/FX energy.
- Clap: trigger a crash, noise hit, or vocal chop.

Avoid mapping Kinect to too many pitch parameters, because it can make the existing song sound out of tune.
